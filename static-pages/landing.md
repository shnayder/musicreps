# Music Reps

**Make music fundamentals automatic so you can focus on playing.**

Music Reps is a fast-drill practice app for guitarists and musicians who want
instant, automatic recall of the fundamentals — fretboard notes, intervals,
scales, chords, key signatures. Short sessions. No account. No ads. No tracking.

## What you practice

- **Fretboard notes** on guitar and ukulele
- **Intervals** — name ↔ semitones, interval math
- **Keys & scales** — key signatures, scale degrees, diatonic chords
- **Chords** — chord spelling and chord shapes

Eleven modes in all. Practice whatever you're weakest at, or let the app pick
for you.

## How it works

Every mode is built around **fast drills with spaced repetition**. The app
tracks how quickly and accurately you answer each item, surfaces the ones you
need to review, and gives you new material only when the stuff you've already
seen is solid.

A short round takes about a minute. The goal isn't long study sessions — it's
many small reps that turn into automatic recall.

## Private by design

Music Reps has no account system, no analytics, no ads, and no network calls.
All your progress is stored on your device. See the
[privacy policy](./privacy.html) for details.
