# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-14_

Music Reps is designed to be usable without accounts, tracking, or data
collection. This policy describes what the app does and does not do with your
information.

## Data we collect

**None.** Music Reps does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on any
server. There is no account system, no analytics, no tracking, and no
advertising.

## Data stored on your device

The app saves the following to your device's local storage so you can pick up
where you left off:

- Per-skill progress (which items you have practiced, response times, accuracy)
- Settings you have chosen (preferred note naming, practice scope, etc.)
- Motor baseline calibration data

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app removes all saved data.

## Permissions

Music Reps does not request access to your microphone, camera, location, or any
other system resource. The only permissions the app uses are those required to
render itself on screen and store data in its own sandbox.

## Third parties

Music Reps does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking
SDKs. Fonts and icons are bundled with the app rather than fetched at runtime.

## Children

Music Reps does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including
children under 13. The app is safe for use by all ages.

## Changes

If this policy changes in a way that affects what data the app collects or how
it uses it, the update will be reflected in this document and the "Last updated"
date above.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the support email listed in the app's
Settings screen.
